# KitchenIQ – Design Guide

## Design Style

Modern, clean and friendly.

The interface should feel welcoming, simple and stress-free for busy students.

---

## Brand Personality

- Helpful
- Smart
- Sustainable
- Friendly
- Minimal

---

## Layout

Mobile-first responsive design.

Navigation:

Home

↓

Ingredient Input

↓

Recipe Results

↓

Recipe Details

---

## Main Screens

### Home

Large hero section

"Cook smarter. Waste less."

Large Get Started button

---

### Ingredient Input

Card layout

Fields:

- Ingredient Name
- Expiry Date
- Quantity
- Dietary Preferences
- Allergies
- Cooking Time

Large green Generate Recipes button.

---

### Recipe Results

Three recipe cards.

Each card contains:

- Image placeholder
- Recipe title
- Cooking time
- Ingredients used
- Expiry score
- "View Recipe" button

Cards should be ranked from best to least suitable.

---

### Recipe Detail

Large recipe image

Ingredients checklist

Step-by-step cooking instructions

Estimated cooking time

Save Recipe button

---

## Icons

Use simple rounded icons.

- 🥦 Ingredients
- ⏰ Cooking Time
- ⚠ Expiring Soon
- ❤️ Favorites

---

## UI Principles

Keep forms short.

Large readable text.

Rounded corners.

Soft shadows.

Lots of white space.

Clear call-to-action buttons.

Accessibility:

- High colour contrast
- Large touch targets
- Simple navigation
